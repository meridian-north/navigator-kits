Meridian North — Navigator Kits

Open, reproducible tools for reading the public record — pharmacovigilance, supplements, and law. Facts and method in the open; the scored signal and full corpora stay private. Each kit is a claim-navigator, not a claim-engine.

Not medical or legal advice. Reports are not rates; a correlation is not a cause; every decision belongs to a person and their clinician or counsel.

Pharmacovigilance & public health

Kennedy–Miller VAERS Review
A six-axis corpus on the 2026 removal of Miller's vaccine–SIDS paper from Toxicology Reports. Days-to-onset sample pull, the SIDS age-confound, a denominated US-vs-Nordic comparison, a fair steelman of Miller's own argument, the outcome-space & latency pitfalls, and a buildable fix. Neutral on safety · strong on method.
vaccine safety · method · corpus
5-Minute VAERS — proof of concept
The data the 30-minute VAERS form asks for already lives in the medical record. One click fills it in — ~30 seconds instead of 30 minutes. Synthetic-data demo, open-source, HIPAA-permitted. A 2011 government project proved this; it was shelved. Why isn't this the national system?
new · demo · open-source
White paper: The Engine Already Exists
Automated vaccine-safety reporting was built and proven on 1.4 million doses in 2011 (ESP:VAERS), then shelved. The underlying engine — ESP — is open-source (BSD-3) and running across 60%+ of Massachusetts today, in a CDC network. We don't need to invent it. We need to fork it.
white paper · open-source · call to action
VAERS Reader — 1990–2026
Ask plain questions of the U.S. vaccine adverse-event record and get the actual rows, in your browser (DuckDB-WASM, nothing leaves the page). Counts of reports, not rates. No denominator. Leads, not verdicts.
query tool · open data

Supplements & cancer

Supplements & Cancer Treatment
The reference implementation: a faceted, evidence-graded index of supplements and therapies in cancer care — interaction signals (WARN/CAUTION/WATCH/LOW/LORE), mechanism crossroads, the question-reshaper, and the "not in corpus" path. Indexed presence is not efficacy.
oncology · supplements · reference

Law

Legal Reference Corpus
The canonical six-axis legal-corpus shell — statute and section index with the who/what/where/when/how/why axes, the question-reshaper, the "not in corpus" fallback, and SHA-256 provenance, in an ink-on-paper formal theme. Not legal advice.
law · statutes · navigator
TickerForum — "We Need ANOTHER Law!"
A six-axis legal-corpus index over a June 2026 Market Ticker post on antitrust enforcement: statute index, enforcement-gap analysis, the "one law leads to another" chain, interactive webform, SHA-256 manifest.
legal · antitrust · preview
