Figure 1 — Enforcement Gap: law on the books vs. law in action
Criminal Sherman §2 prosecutions: 100+ in the Act's first ~80 years, then none 1977→2022; the two revival cases (Zito 2022, fuel-truck 2024) ended in probation, not decade terms. Left: binding criminal/structural law already in force (WARN — near-zero criminal use). Right: the fresh bills proposed to address the same conduct (WATCH — not law).
